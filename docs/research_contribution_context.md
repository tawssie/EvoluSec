# Research Contribution Context

This page provides a factual clarification of my formally documented research contributions, particularly in relation to research work that was formally incorporated into my doctoral thesis at The University of Queensland (UQ). It is intended to help readers evaluate my work on the basis of verifiable contribution records, technical substance, and formal research history, rather than on superficial assumptions based only on authorship position. UQ’s Higher Degree by Research Examination Guideline states that, for scholarly work included in a thesis, the candidate must have made a substantial contribution to the conception and design of the project, the analysis and interpretation of the research data, and the drafting and production of significant parts of the work; the candidate must also have participated sufficiently to take public responsibility for the work, and a clear statement of authorship and contribution must be provided in the preliminary pages of the thesis. (Policy and Procedure Library)

## Why this page exists

Authorship position does not always fully convey the nature or extent of a researcher’s contribution. In my case, my doctoral thesis formally documented the substantial academic and technical contributions I made to the research reported in *NAD+ cleavage activity by animal and plant TIR domains in cell death pathways* (DOI: 10.1126/science.aax1911). This page provides concise, record-based context so that readers can interpret my contribution history through what was formally documented in my doctoral thesis and through the underlying research work, rather than through author order alone. The same UQ guideline also makes clear that works published prior to candidature cannot be included in a doctoral thesis, all authors must agree to inclusion, and scholarly works included in a doctoral thesis must form part of a coherent thesis argument rather than being attached casually. (Policy and Procedure Library)

## What is formally documented

My doctoral thesis formally incorporated multiple research outputs to which I made substantial contributions. Under UQ policy, the basis for including scholarly work in a doctoral thesis is substantive contribution, not merely nominal authorship. The university requires that the candidate have made a substantial contribution to project conception and design, data analysis and interpretation, and the writing of significant parts of each scholarly work included in the doctoral thesis, together with a clear authorship and contribution statement. (Policy and Procedure Library)

In practical terms, my contribution included central methodological and analytical work rather than peripheral assistance. One major part of that work involved the systematic extraction of sequence and structural information from the Protein Data Bank database using stringent quality criteria, followed by the construction of a secondary-structure transition analysis framework on that basis. Using current AI/ML terminology, this can reasonably be described as the creation of a high-quality ground-truth dataset and a ground-truth evolutionary probability framework derived from stringent structural evidence.

More broadly, this work also illustrates why close collaboration between structural bioinformatics and wet-lab research can be highly important. In that sense, my doctoral thesis reflects a strong collaborative project outcome: the development of new structure-based analytical tools, the identification of new functional directions from structural evidence, and the subsequent experimental examination of those biological implications.

## Clarification on authorship position

Authorship order alone does not fully reflect research contribution. This is especially true in projects where one contributor is responsible for building core datasets, designing analytical frameworks, carrying out large-scale computational analysis, or generating key evidence that made later scientific interpretation possible. My purpose here is not to deny standard authorship conventions, but to clarify that, in my case, the formal record of my doctoral thesis already documented that my contribution was substantial and central.

Accordingly, the fact that I was not always listed as first author should not be misread as evidence that I was only a minor participant. The proper basis for evaluating my research work is the formally documented research record, including the contribution statements in my doctoral thesis and the technical work I actually carried out.

## Why the thesis was under embargo

Parts of my doctoral thesis were placed under embargo because they involved collaborative research materials that were not yet public at the time, including experimental results and related analysis concerning dSARM1-ARM, NMN, and NAD+. Because these materials formed part of an ongoing collaborative research context and may have been connected to later publication and data-disclosure arrangements, they were not suitable for full public release at the time of thesis submission. The purpose of this explanation is only to clarify the background and reason why the thesis was placed under embargo at the time of submission. A later public study related to this research direction is *SARM1 is a metabolic sensor activated by an increased NMN/NAD+ ratio to trigger axon degeneration* (Neuron, 2021), DOI: 10.1016/j.neuron.2021.02.009; however, I am not asserting here that the contents of that paper and the materials in my thesis were the same original dataset. The publicly available content of that paper shows that its research topic involved SARM1, the NMN/NAD+ ratio, and mechanisms related to the ARM domain.

## What I want this page to make clear

This page makes four points.

First, my research contribution history should be evaluated on the basis of formal records and verifiable evidence, rather than on simplified assumptions drawn only from author order.

Second, the inclusion of scholarly work in a UQ doctoral thesis is itself subject to explicit formal requirements, including that the candidate must have made a substantial contribution and that clear contribution statements must be provided. (Policy and Procedure Library)

Third, one important part of my work consisted of sustained and methodologically central research activity: stringent structural data curation, the derivation and exploration of secondary-structure transition probabilities, the development of evolutionary computational analysis tools, and the construction of a rigorous analytical framework from large-scale structural evidence. This line of work ultimately led to a focused structural analysis of TIR domains and their role in innate immunological signalling.

Fourth, the embargo status of the doctoral thesis reflected the presence of restricted material, not the absence of contribution.

## What this page is not

This page is not a personal complaint against any individual, institution, or interviewer. It is not a claim that authorship conventions are meaningless, and it is not a request for special treatment. It also does not attempt to reconstruct every internal step of the later research process or to assign exclusive credit for subsequent experiments. Its purpose is narrower and factual: to provide a clear, document-based account of my research contribution in a manner consistent with the formal doctoral thesis record and the actual technical work performed. The formal doctoral thesis record may not capture every internal decision made during later project development, but it does establish that my contribution to the underlying research was substantive rather than nominal.

## Closing statement

I hope this page helps readers interpret my work on the basis of documented contribution, technical substance, and verifiable academic record. My contribution was substantive, methodologically central, and clearly documented in my doctoral thesis.

# 研究貢獻說明

本頁旨在就我已正式記錄的研究貢獻提供一份基於事實的澄清，特別是針對那些已被正式納入我在昆士蘭大學（The University of Queensland, UQ）博士論文中的研究工作。設立本頁的目的，是希望讀者能根據可驗證的貢獻紀錄、技術實質內容，以及正式研究歷程來評估我的工作，而不是僅僅根據作者排序所形成的表面印象。UQ 的高等學位研究考試指引明確指出，若要將學術成果納入博士論文，候選人必須在研究構想與設計、研究資料的分析與詮釋，以及作品重要部分的撰寫與完成上具有實質貢獻；候選人也必須參與到足以對該成果公開負責的程度，並且必須在博士論文前置頁面中清楚列出作者及貢獻說明。

## 為什麼我寫這一頁

作者排序並不一定能完整傳達研究者貢獻的性質與程度。就我的情況而言，我的博士論文正式記錄了我在 NAD+ cleavage activity by animal and plant TIR domains in cell death pathways (DOI: 10.1126/science.aax1911) 研究中所作出的實質性學術與技術貢獻。設立本頁，是希望任何審閱我背景的人都能根據博士論文中正式記錄的內容及其背後的研究工作，較完整地理解我的貢獻歷程，而不是僅憑作者排序來推斷。UQ 的同一份指引也明確指出，候選人入學前已發表的成果不得納入博士論文，所有作者都必須同意納入，而納入博士論文的學術成果也必須構成整體博士論文論證的一部分，而不是隨意附加。

## 有哪些內容是正式記錄的

我的博士論文正式納入了多項我具有實質貢獻的研究成果。依照 UQ 的規範，學術成果能被納入博士論文，所依據的是實質貢獻，而不是名義上的掛名作者身分。校方要求候選人在研究構想與設計、資料分析與詮釋，以及每一項納入博士論文的學術成果之重要部分撰寫上，都必須具有實質貢獻，並且必須附上清楚的作者與貢獻說明。

就實際內容而言，我的貢獻包含了具關鍵性的研究方法與分析工作，而非邊緣性的協助。其中一項重要工作，是我以嚴格品質標準，系統性地從 Protein Data Bank（PDB）資料庫中抽取序列與結構資訊，並在此基礎上建立 secondary-structure transition 的分析框架。若用今日 AI/ML 的語言來描述，這可合理視為建立了一套高品質的 ground-truth dataset，以及一個基於嚴格結構證據所推導出的 ground-truth evolutionary probability framework。

更廣義地說，這份工作也體現了 structural bioinformatics 與 wet-lab 研究之間緊密合作的重要性。從這個角度來看，我的博士論文呈現的是一份完整且具代表性的合作研究成果：一方面開發新的結構分析工具，另一方面根據結構證據辨識出值得進一步探討的功能方向，並由後續實驗進一步檢驗其生物學意涵。

## 關於作者排序的說明

作者排序本身，並不能完整反映研究貢獻。尤其在某些研究中，一位研究者可能負責建構核心資料集、設計分析框架、執行大規模計算分析，或產生使後續科學詮釋成為可能的關鍵證據。在這些情況下，作者排序並不足以完整呈現其實際角色。我的目的不是否定一般作者排序慣例，而是要說明：就我的情況而言，博士論文的正式記錄本身已清楚記載，我的貢獻是實質且核心的。

因此，我並非總是列為第一作者，這件事不應被誤讀為我只是次要參與者。對我研究工作的恰當評價基礎，應該是正式記錄的研究證據，包括博士論文中的貢獻說明，以及我實際完成的技術內容。

## 為什麼博士論文需要延後公開

我博士論文中的部分內容之所以採取延後公開處理，是因為其中涉及當時尚未公開的合作研究材料，包括與 dSARM1-ARM、NMN 與 NAD+ 相關的實驗結果及分析內容。由於這些內容屬於當時仍在發展中的合作研究脈絡，且可能涉及後續正式發表與資料揭露安排，因此在論文提交當時不適合完整對外公開。此處說明的目的，僅在於交代當時採取延後公開的背景與原因。後續與此研究方向相關的公開研究可參見 SARM1 is a metabolic sensor activated by an increased NMN/NAD+ ratio to trigger axon degeneration (Neuron, 2021), DOI: 10.1016/j.neuron.2021.02.009；惟我在此並不主張該文內容與我論文中的材料為同一份原始資料。該文公開內容顯示其研究主題涉及 SARM1、NMN/NAD+ 比值，以及與 ARM domain 相關的機制。

## 我希望這一頁說清楚的事

本頁想表達四點。

第一，我的研究貢獻歷程應該根據正式記錄與可驗證證據來評估，而不是僅僅依據作者排序所形成的簡化推定。

第二，UQ 博士論文中納入學術成果的前提，本來就包含明確且正式的要求，也就是候選人必須具有實質貢獻，並附有清楚的貢獻說明。

第三，我的重要工作之一，是投入大量時間與方法學心力，完成嚴格的結構資料整理、secondary-structure transition probability 的推導與探索、演化計算分析工具的開發，以及從大規模結構證據中建立嚴格的分析框架。這條研究路線最終聚焦於 TIR domain 的結構分析，並進一步探討 TIR 在 innate immunological signalling 中的作用機制。

第四，博士論文的延後公開狀態，反映的是其中包含受限制的資料，而不是我缺乏貢獻。

## 本頁不是什麼

本頁並不是對任何個人、機構或面試官的私人抱怨。它也不是主張作者排序完全沒有意義，更不是要求特殊待遇。本頁也不試圖重建後續研究過程中的每一個內部決策步驟，亦不主張對後續實驗擁有排他性的歸功權。本頁的目的更單純也更明確：依據正式博士論文紀錄與我實際完成的技術工作，提供一份清楚、可查證的研究貢獻說明。正式博士論文紀錄未必能完整呈現後續研究發展中的每一個內部決策，但它已足以說明我的貢獻屬於實質性貢獻，而非名義上的掛名。

## 結語

我希望這一頁能幫助讀者根據正式記錄的貢獻、技術實質內容，以及可驗證的學術紀錄來理解我的工作。我的貢獻屬於實質性且方法學核心的工作，並且已在博士論文中明確記錄。
